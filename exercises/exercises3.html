The XX Website Analysis

  1. Describe the fonts used as headers, paragraphs, and pull quotes.

    2. The serif font used gave the headers and paragraphs a nice visual style which was easy to read. Did not understand why a sans serif font was used with the first pull quote and the others had a serif font. Also, why have the mail chimp pull quote come in near the middle of the page and not at the end to get props for the web page. I did like that the paragraphs along with the visuals were placed in linear form.

  2. Describe the color palette. What colors are used? Where and how are specific colors used?

    3. The color palette is black and white with some blue and burnt red. The layout is simple but effective, and has a consistent colour theme that does not distract you from the content. The burnt red color used was nicely used in a couple of the quotes as well as the visual video and group photo collage. The one photo with the blue background should have been consistent with the other photos / visual cue photos using the faded black and white background.

  3. What role do the visuals play? When and how are visuals used?

    4. The visuals gave this page a way of expressing the group as being a close family as well as being childhood friends. While the individual visual video gave you a closer look and hint of each persons personality. The quality of some of the visuals were a little grainy which did give it some flavor.

  4. How are sections separated? What are the visual cues?

    5. The visuals on this page were used as a break between the paragraphs along with the pull quotes. Using the photos and video-photos as the visual cues to keep you focused on the group.
