Interactive Design 1 - Spring 2017

My process for creating my wireframe for my favorite musical artist project, was to know and have a little background and lifestyle information about Al Jarreau. I then drew my wireframe accordingly to include color, font, and images of the artist I chose.

I tryed to show the content of hierarchy by using a abstract photo as my background folowed by a photo of the artist and several album covers placed as a divison along with the text.
